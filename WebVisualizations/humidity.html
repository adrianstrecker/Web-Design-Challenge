Humidity


Based on the scatter plot distribution generated, there does not appear to be a correlation between latitude and humidity. Both the northern and southern hemispheres experience varying amounts of humidity. We see there is grouping of lower humidity closer to the northern latitude line and southern latitude line, but despite this we also see higher levels of humididty which indicates there is no real correlation between humidity and latitude. When calculating the pearson r value we found this confirmed a lack of correlation as well so it is safe to say latitude has no effect on humidity in a given location.
