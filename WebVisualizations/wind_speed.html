Wind Speed


Based on the scatter plot generated from the data, we see most points are on the lower end of speed in the northern hemisphere and southern hemisphere. There is no real grouping seen at a given latitude; because of this, wind speed does not appear to be correlated to latitude. This is probably due to other factors affecting wind speed including things such as geological formations like mountains and valleys. Ocean currents may also play a role as we know certain areas are more prone to storms such as thunderstorms and hurricanes.
